Thank You u-13

Published in June 29th, 2025

Today, June 28th, I conclude my time at Atlético Clube Marinhense, but of course, I couldn't leave without thanking those who helped me the most.

"More than an internship, it was a family that I got to know."

It was thanks to the U-13s at ACM that I was able to grow, and of course, make new friends, and progress in my work as a photographer.

It all started with a simple photography internship, but in the end, it was a boom. It was thanks to these people that I finally decided, once and for all, what I wanted to pursue in life. And of course, it was also thanks to them that I became a better person.
