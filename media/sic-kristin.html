Kristin Storm @ SIC Notícias Interview

A first-person account of support for affected populations.

Impact on the Ground

During the passage of Kristin Storm, I volunteered in aid operations. In this SIC Notícias interview, at the request of Rúben Antunes and João Massapina (journalist and videographer), I share the challenges experienced on the ground and the difficulties faced.

"Solidarity was our best response to the storm."
