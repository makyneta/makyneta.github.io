Website For The Musician, Ice J

João Antunes 1 week

Overview & Purpose

This project aims to bring together the entire catalog of musician João Antunes, under the artistic name Ice J, where it is intended to gather albums, singles, EPs, live performances, among others. The website also contains the dates of live performances, along with music videos, and a brief introduction about who the musician behind the project is. It also includes a contact form, along with redirectable icons for social media and streaming platforms to listen to the artist. All of this is incorporated into a professional, minimalist design.
