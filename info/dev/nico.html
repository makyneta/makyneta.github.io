Overview & Purpose

This project aims to create a portfolio-bio for the Brazilian photographer, Nicholas Simões. This website will provide an organized and modern overview of Nicholas's background, his work, a brief portfolio showcasing his best and most professional projects, client feedback, and a short contact section.
