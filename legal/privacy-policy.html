02 —

Data We Collect

Depending on how you interact with Makyneta, we may collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Contact data — name, email address, phone number, provided voluntarily via contact forms or direct communication
  • Project data — information you share as part of a service engagement (briefs, references, feedback)
  • Newsletter data — name and email address if you subscribe to our newsletter
  • Usage data — anonymised browsing data such as pages visited, browser type, and device type, collected via analytics tools
  • Photography — images in which you may appear, taken at events or in public contexts where Makyneta was commissioned

We do not collect sensitive personal data (as defined under Art. 9 GDPR) unless strictly necessary and with your explicit consent.

03 —

Why We Collect It

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To respond to enquiries and provide the services you requested
  • To manage contractual relationships and deliver project work
  • To send newsletters or updates, if you have subscribed
  • To improve the website and user experience through anonymised analytics
  • To comply with legal obligations applicable in Portugal and the EU

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

04 —

Legal Basis for Processing

Under the GDPR (Art. 6), we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — for newsletter subscriptions and non-essential cookies
  • Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) — when processing is necessary to fulfil a service agreement
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) — for website analytics and business communications
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)) — where required by Portuguese or EU law
05 —

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this policy, or as required by applicable law:

  • Contact and project data: retained for the duration of the project plus up to 5 years, in compliance with Portuguese commercial law (Art. 40.º do Código Comercial)
  • Newsletter data: retained until you unsubscribe
  • Analytics data: retained in anonymised form for up to 26 months

After the applicable retention period, data is securely deleted or permanently anonymised.

We do not transfer your data to any other third parties without your explicit consent.

07 —

Your Rights

Under the GDPR and Portuguese law (Lei n.º 58/2019), you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access (Art. 15) — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16) — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17) — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restriction (Art. 18) — request limitation of processing in certain circumstances
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20) — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object (Art. 21) — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent — at any time, for consent-based processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at makyneta@tutamail.com. We will respond within 30 days as required by Art. 12 GDPR.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Portuguese data protection authority, the CNPD (Comissão Nacional de Proteção de Dados): www.cnpd.pt.

08 —

Cookies

This website may use cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve your browsing experience and analyse usage patterns.

  • Essential cookies — necessary for the website to function; no consent required
  • Analytics cookies — used to understand how visitors interact with the site; require consent

In accordance with the Portuguese Electronic Communications Law (Lei n.º 41/2004, de 18 de agosto, as amended by Lei n.º 46/2012), non-essential cookies are only placed with your prior consent.

This website does not currently use third-party advertising cookies.
