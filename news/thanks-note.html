Tomás Mota

I write this note of gratitude on a personal level, to everyone who helped — and continues to help — Marinha Grande in its time of reconstruction, following Storm Kristin.

Over these 23 days as a volunteer, it was deeply moving to witness hundreds of thousands of people coming together to help our municipality rebuild. Young people, the elderly, the vulnerable, military personnel — from all across the country, north to south. Not to mention the thousands of donations from anonymous individuals, businesses, and people from across mainland Portugal, Spain, and France.

Without you, none of this would have been possible.

I also want to extend my gratitude to the mayor, Paulo Vicente, and his team, for their professionalism in coordinating and directing the volunteer groups — making everything run smoothly. My thanks go equally to every member of the municipal council, without exception.

I'm starting a new chapter next week, but I close this one with a mission that is fulfilled — though not yet finished. I have no doubt that I made lasting friendships, both professional and personal. Above all, I feel that I helped, to the best of my ability, our beloved municipality of Marinha Grande.

Municipal Storage Yard

Over the coming weeks I will continue visiting the yards to help out — something I genuinely love doing. From Monday, 23 February 2026, the municipal storage yard will operate from 09:00 to 12:30, and from 14:00 to 17:00, by appointment only.

Appointments should be requested by email at apoio.social@cm-mgrande.pt. The message must include the specific material required (for example, roof tile SOL 10, or ridge tile MARCELHA), along with your name and phone number. If the material is in stock, you will be contacted by phone with the collection date and time.

For those without access to email, you are welcome to go directly to the municipal yard and provide the same information to the person on-site.

For more information, visit: makyneta.github.io/tempestade-kristin.

I close this letter with a heartfelt thank you — to EVERY ONE of you who made this possible. Thank you from the bottom of my heart. Together, we will rebuild Marinha Grande! 🩵
