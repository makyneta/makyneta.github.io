Tomás Mota

A special thank you note to the JS, and especially to Catarina Carlos from the JS of Marinha Grande, for providing me with this visit even though it was at the last minute.

On March 16, I contacted the Leiria Federation of the JS, who quickly redirected me to contact Catarina Carlos from the JS of Marinha Grande - she responded quickly and fortunately (even at the last minute), I had the honor of joining the Socialist Youth of Marinha Grande, and fortunately, thanks to Catarina, I had the honor of being able to make this same visit. It took place on March 19, which included a visit to the Parliament of the Republic, along with viewing a piece of the initial Plenary Session, followed by a visit to the Socialist Party Headquarters. A special thank you also to Ricardo Abreu, president of the Federation, for accepting and for the trust. Personally, I can only be grateful, as it was a unique experience that certainly makes me even more confident in defending the Socialist Party. We were 33 militants from various municipalities of Leiria (Marinha Grande, Leiria, Ansião, Pombal, and Bombarral), and it was a unique experience, which even allowed me to make new friends. I will continue with a carnation on my chest, defending the JS and the PS, and fighting for a better Portugal, and even more just. Together, my promise follows, and I will continue with the Socialist Youth of Marinha Grande, helping always and forever. Thank you all very much.
