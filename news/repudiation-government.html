Letter of repudiation to the Government of Portugal, especially to Minister Manuel Castro Almeida, due to the accusations made.

Last week, Minister Manuel Castro Almeida attributed the delay in evaluating applications for the reconstruction of homes affected by Storm Kristin on January 28th to local authorities, and stressed the importance of a thorough analysis of support forms, even for simple support, to avoid repeating the mistakes made during the Pedrógão Grande fire in October 2017 – this despite the fact that not only in the municipality of Marinha Grande, but also in the district of Leiria, there are people without internet access, and especially telecommunications. Meanwhile, fuel prices rose by 10 cents last week alone and will rise again by 10 cents next Monday. I wonder if the government is making a mockery of us. I have maintained contact with the Mayor of Marinha Grande, Paulo Vicente, the Mayor of Leiria, Gonçalo Lopes, and the Mayor of Ferreira do Zêzere, Bruno Gomes – I have been helping the Municipality of Marinha Grande, where I reside, and constant hard work has been done. These accusations from the government serve as an excuse for delaying action, as has also happened with E-Redes regarding the restoration of power. On March 11th, the Mayor of Marinha Grande, Paulo Vicente, emphasized that "these statements do not correspond to the reality experienced on the ground" and also stressed that "municipalities are working to the limit of their capacities, combining the analysis of applications with the recovery of infrastructure, direct support to the population, and the management of the emergency situation," this to remind us that the municipalities have been fully mobilized to support the population from the first moment after the storm, despite the procedures and rules having been defined by the Government of Portugal. Finally, I would like to inform you that the Municipality of Marinha Grande has resumed the distribution of construction materials to the population at the municipal construction site from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM until March 31st. No prior request is necessary; simply go to the construction site. I end this letter by sending a message of support to everyone affected by Storm Kristin and a big hug to the mayors for all the work done with their respective executives, a special hug to the president of the Marinha Grande Municipal Council, Paulo Vicente. Together we will rebuild and do better! 🩵
