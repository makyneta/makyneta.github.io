Tomás Mota

Public statement regarding Tropical Storm Kristin, which struck Marinha Grande, causing significant damage and loss of life.

During this past week and a half, I have been helping the municipality at the shipyards (which I will continue to do), and I have no doubt that the Navy is more united than ever! These have been and will be intense days, and together we will be able to rebuild the Navy even stronger. I thank everyone, without exception, who has made it possible to rebuild our municipality. A thank you also to Mayor Paulo Vicente for the excellent and exhaustive work he has done, because without him it would not have been possible to get where we are and we will get even further. I, personally, will continue to help whenever I can, because, in my view, we must all unite more and more to be able to rebuild the municipality not only faster, but better!

If you want to make donations, or need to collect food, construction materials, tarpaulins, plastic sheeting, roof tiles, etc., and also if you want to volunteer, visit the page by clicking here. I have attached the damage assessment form, which you can complete in paper form at the town hall or online at: https://www.cm-mgrande.pt/p/tempestade.
